Conditions & needs we support.

Tap any card to learn more. Whatever the label, we see the child first.

Autism Spectrum Disorder

+

Strengths-based support for communication, sensory needs, social skills and routines — with therapy that feels like play and a plan that fits your child.

ADHD & Attention

+

Practical strategies for focus, impulse control and self-regulation — at home and at school — alongside confidence-building and parent coaching.

Speech & Language Delay

+

From late talkers to unclear speech, we build understanding and expression step by step — so your child can finally say what they mean.

Learning Disabilities

+

Dyslexia, dysgraphia, dyscalculia and more — taught through a structured, multisensory approach matched to how your child learns best.

Developmental Delay

+

When milestones are slower to arrive, early, coordinated intervention makes the biggest difference. We help your child catch their stride.

Sensory Processing

+

For children overwhelmed or under-responsive to sound, touch and movement, sensory integration brings calm, focus and comfort in daily life.

Motor & Coordination

+

Gross and fine motor challenges affect everything from handwriting to play. Physiotherapy and OT build strength, balance and coordination.

Behavioural & Emotional

+

Big feelings, meltdowns, anxiety and rigidity are forms of communication. We help children regulate and cope — with warmth, never shame.

Meet Dr Khushboo Gour

Parents don't choose a centre. They choose a person they can trust with their child.

Dr Khushboo Gour
Founder & Clinical Director

Dr Khushboo founded AAKAR with one conviction, earned over years of working with children and their families: no child is broken. Every child simply has their own pace, their own path, and their own extraordinary way of growing.

She built AAKAR to be the place she always wished existed — where assessments feel like understanding, not labelling; where therapy feels like play; and where parents are partners, never spectators.

Where is your child?

Every age has its own milestones.

Find your child's stage to see what's typical, what's worth watching, and how AAKAR helps.

The foundation years

Babbling, first words, pointing, walking, simple play.

Worth watching for
  • Few or no words by 18–24 months
  • Little eye contact or response to name
  • Not pointing, waving or imitating
  • Delays in sitting, crawling or walking
How AAKAR helps

The discovery years

Sentences, pretend play, sharing, early independence.

Worth watching for
  • Speech that's hard to understand
  • Trouble following simple instructions
  • Difficulty playing or sharing with peers
  • Big, frequent meltdowns or rigidity

The school years

Reading, writing, friendships, focus, confidence.

Worth watching for
  • Struggles with reading, writing or maths
  • Trouble sitting still or paying attention
  • Few friendships or social difficulties
  • Low confidence or school refusal

Worth watching for
