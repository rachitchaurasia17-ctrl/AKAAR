A small story

Watch a child unfold — one ability at a time.

Development isn't a straight line. As your child grows, AAKAR meets them at every turn — gently turning struggle into skill.

01

First, we understand.

A gentle developmental assessment shows exactly where your child is — and what they're ready for next. No labels.

02

We help them connect.

Speech & language therapy turns frustration into first words — and first words into whole conversations.

03

We help them move.

From holding a crayon to climbing stairs — occupational therapy & physio build the strength everyday confidence needs.

04

We help them learn.

Some minds need a different doorway in. Special education meets your child exactly where they think.

05

We help them belong.

Social skills & behaviour support help your child feel at home in the world — and in a room full of children.

06

We help them thrive.

The goal was never therapy — it was independence. Life-skills & ADL training hand the world back to your child.

The heart behind AAKAR

Meet Dr Khushboo Gour

Parents don't choose a centre. They choose a person they can trust with their child.

Dr Khushboo Gour
Founder & Clinical Director

Dr Khushboo founded AAKAR with one conviction, earned over years of working with children and their families: no child is broken. Every child simply has their own pace, their own path, and their own extraordinary way of growing.

She built AAKAR to be the place she always wished existed — where assessments feel like understanding, not labelling; where therapy feels like play; and where parents are partners, never spectators.

Years of experience
Hands-on work across assessment, therapy and family guidance.
Child-first philosophy
Plans built around the child — never the other way around.
Multidisciplinary lens
Speech, OT, psychology & education, working as one team.
Families as partners
Coaching parents so progress continues at home, too.
